About Petrocrib

Born in Kerala, built on dialogues. Petrocrib started with a simple idea — the scenes and one-liners we grew up quoting deserve more than forwarded stickers. They deserve to be worn. Every design begins as a moment we all know by heart, reimagined as art you can pull over your head and walk out in.

We print on quality fabric in regular, oversized and hoodie fits, ship free across India, and drop new designs whenever the culture demands it. This isn't fast fashion — it's a love letter to Malayalam cinema and internet culture, one tee at a time. Wear the dialogue. Set the standard.
